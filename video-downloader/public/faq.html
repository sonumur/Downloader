🛠️ Troubleshooting
Why am I getting an "unsupported URL" error?▾

This error appears when the link is not from a supported platform, or the link is malformed. Make sure you are copying the link using the Share > Copy Link feature inside the app — not typing it manually. The link should start with https://www.instagram.com/, https://www.tiktok.com/, https://www.facebook.com/, or https://twitter.com/ / https://x.com/.

Why does the download take a long time or get stuck?▾

For large, high-quality files (like 4K or long HD videos), processing can take 30–90 seconds. If the page appears stuck for more than 2 minutes, try refreshing and attempting again. Also check your internet connection stability. On first use, our server may take a few extra seconds to "wake up" if it hasn't been used recently.

My downloaded video has no sound. What do I do?▾

Some high-resolution video formats store video and audio in separate streams. If you downloaded a format that says "(no sound)", try selecting a different format option from the dropdown — look for options without that label, or choose a slightly lower resolution like 720p. These are typically pre-combined (video + audio in one file) and play perfectly.

I get a "video is private" or "restricted" error. Can it be fixed?▾

Unfortunately, no. If the video belongs to a private account, has been set to "Friends only" on Facebook, or has been deleted/taken down by the platform, we cannot access it. Our servers can only process content that is publicly visible without logging in. This is by design.

The download finishes but I can't find the file on my phone.▾

Check these locations depending on your device:

  • Android: Open your "Files" or "My Files" app and look in the "Downloads" folder, or check your Downloads notification in the status bar.
  • iPhone: Open the Files app → Browse → On My iPhone → Downloads. The file may appear as a .mp4 file. Long-press it to "Save to Photo Library" if you want it in your Camera Roll.
  • Chrome/Android: Tap the three-dot menu → Downloads to see all recent downloads.
