About downloader.online

We believe saving the content you love should be instant, free, and completely private — no apps, no accounts, no nonsense.

Our Story

downloader.online was born out of a simple frustration: saving a video from social media was far harder than it needed to be. Between sketchy apps, shady browser extensions, sites loaded with malware pop-ups, and tools that watermark your downloads or lock premium quality behind a paywall — the landscape was a mess.

We set out to build something different: a clean, honest, fast video downloader that works the way users expect it to. No bait-and-switch, no hidden fees, no deceptive prompts. Just paste a link, pick your quality, and download. That principle has guided every decision we've made since launch.

Today, downloader.online supports downloading from Instagram (Reels, Stories, Photos, and Posts), TikTok (without watermark), Facebook, Twitter/X, and more — with new platforms being added regularly as we continue to grow and improve.

"Our mission is to make high-quality video saving accessible to everyone — from the casual user who wants to save a funny clip they found online, to the content creator archiving their own work."

What Makes Us Different

The internet is full of video downloader sites, but most of them share common problems. We've worked hard to address every one of them:

  • We never add our own watermarks or branding to your downloaded files
  • We never redirect you to a fake "virus protection" or "update required" page
  • We never ask you to complete a survey or human verification to start a download
  • We never create fake countdown timers or artificial delays to inflate engagement
  • We clearly label which formats include audio and which don't — no surprises
  • We always show the real available quality options — we never upsell or hide formats
  • Our download button is always the real download button — no disguised ad buttons
  • We delete your temporary files from our servers within 24 hours automatically

Responsible Use Policy

We are strong advocates for respecting intellectual property. downloader.online is designed to help you save content for personal offline viewing, archive your own uploaded content, or save videos you have explicit permission to use.

We actively discourage using our tool to pirate content, re-upload others' work without credit, or use downloaded media for commercial purposes without permission. Every time you download a creator's work, we encourage you to engage with their original content — like it, follow them, and support their creative efforts.

If you believe your content is being misused and you'd like to request its removal, please visit our DMCA page for instructions on submitting a copyright complaint.

Transparency & Advertising

downloader.online is entirely funded by display advertising. We believe in complete transparency about this: you will see non-intrusive ads on our pages. These ads allow us to pay for servers, development, and support — so we can keep the service free and ad-free in terms of our own branding.

We work exclusively with Google's AdSense advertising program, which has strict controls over ad content quality. We do not accept direct advertising deals from unverified parties, and we do not allow pop-under ads, auto-play audio ads, or any other formats known to disrupt the user experience.
